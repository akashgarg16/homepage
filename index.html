About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher in astrophysics, specializing in the study of accretion dynamics and radiative processes in X-ray binaries. My work focuses on spectral-timing and polarization analyses using data from missions like IXPE, AstroSat, and NICER.

Research Interests

  • Accretion physics in black hole X-ray binaries
  • X-ray polarization and spectro-temporal modeling
  • Multi-wavelength observations
  • Application of machine learning in high-energy astrophysics
